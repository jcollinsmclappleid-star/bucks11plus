HERO & BRAND REFINEMENT – INSTITUTIONAL AUTHORITY DIRECTION

OBJECTIVE
Refine the visual identity to feel like an independent assessment authority,
not a tutoring website or school prospectus.

Do NOT introduce literal school buildings or photography.
Do NOT add aspirational stock imagery.

The tone must feel:
Measured.
Structured.
Calm.
Institutional.
Independent.

--------------------------------------------------------
1. HERO BACKGROUND REFINEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------

Remove:
- Lens flare
- Bright glow streaks
- Overly tech-style lighting effects

Keep:
- Deep navy base

Enhance with:
- Subtle structured texture overlay (3–5% opacity)
  Options:
    • Fine academic grid pattern
    • Very faint exam-paper horizontal lines
    • Soft geometric line architecture motif (non-literal)

Texture must:
- Be monochrome
- Be barely noticeable
- Never compete with text
- Add depth, not decoration

Add:
- Very soft radial light gradient behind headline only
- Slight vignette toward outer edges

No animation.
No parallax.
Static only.

--------------------------------------------------------
2. COLOUR CALIBRATION
--------------------------------------------------------

Primary background:
Deep midnight navy (#0B1C2D–#0F2236 range)

Accent:
Muted amber (less orange, more restrained gold)
Avoid bright or saturated tones.

Goal:
Academic, not startup.

--------------------------------------------------------
3. LOGO REFINEMENT (INCREASE GRAVITAS)
--------------------------------------------------------

Typography structure:

Line 1:
11+  (larger, heavier weight)

Line 2:
STANDARD  (small caps OR increased letter spacing + lighter weight)

Increase:
- Letter spacing slightly on STANDARD
- Vertical spacing between lines

Icon:
Keep circular precision motif.
Increase stroke thickness slightly.
Simplify internal details if overly intricate.
It should feel closer to a seal than an app icon.

No gradients inside logo.
Flat, strong, confident.

--------------------------------------------------------
4. HEADER MICRO POSITIONING (OPTIONAL BUT RECOMMENDED)
--------------------------------------------------------

Under logo in hero only, small restrained line:

“Bucks 11+ Readiness & Forecast”

Low contrast.
Subtle.
Not dominant.

Purpose:
Clarifies positioning instantly.

--------------------------------------------------------
5. TYPOGRAPHY REFINEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------

Headlines:
Increase line-height slightly for authority.
Avoid overly tight kerning.

Body:
Keep readable, not light-weight.

Avoid:
Playful font weights.
Overly rounded fonts.

Tone:
Institutional sans-serif.

--------------------------------------------------------
6. REMOVE VISUAL ELEMENTS THAT FEEL:
--------------------------------------------------------

- EdTech startup
- Private tutor marketing
- School brochure
- Overly emotional
- Overly animated

This is an independent readiness modelling system.

--------------------------------------------------------
7. FINAL CHECK CRITERIA
--------------------------------------------------------

When viewing hero, it should feel like:

An assessment body.
A structured readiness standard.
A measured analytical system.

If it feels like:
“Admissions marketing” → too aspirational.
“Startup SaaS” → too tech.
“Tutoring flyer” → too soft.

We want:
Calm authority.
